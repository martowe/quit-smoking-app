Quitting Smoking Tips

You have made a great decision to give up smoking. Here are some effective strategies to help you succeed on your journey to becoming smoke-free.

1 Once you have picked your quit date, remember to add it to your calendar.
2 List your reasons to quit and keep them visible for daily motivation.
3 Tell people you're quitting to build a support network.
4 If you have tried to quit before, remember what worked and learn from what didn't.
5 Use stop smoking aids like nicotine replacement therapy if needed.
6 Have a plan if you are tempted to smoke - identify coping strategies in advance.
7 List your smoking triggers and how to avoid them.
8 Keep cravings at bay by keeping busy with new activities and hobbies.
9 Exercise away the urge - physical activity reduces cravings and improves mood.
10 Throw away all your cigarettes, lighters, and ashtrays before you start.
11 Remember, there is never "just 1 cigarette". You can do it!
